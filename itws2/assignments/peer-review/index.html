1 Objective

The objective of this activity is to review and enhance the quality of the documents compiled in the Bonus Activity Part-1. This is being done to make the documents compiled in Part 1 of publishable quality. The students need to:

  • re-frame sentences if they are meaningless in the current state (or)
  • restructure/ reorganize sections (or)
  • scan for spelling mistakes, grammatical mistakes and factual errors as well

Added bonus:

  • You earn points for revising topics for your lab exam !
  • It is a simple task and does not take long for reviewing written content.

NOTE: Students are NOT allowed to add new content to the existing material. This activity strictly focuses on enhancing the collected content.

Marking Scheme:

  • reframing & restructuring: +3, -0
  • spelling/grammatical mistakes: +4, -2
  • factual error: +10, -5

The negative marks will be given to the team who made the commit and positive marks to the team suggesting the corrections.

4 Submission Instructions

IMPORTANT NOTE
Each group needs to co-ordinate and decide only one person to be the one to create a merge request.
  1. Each group is given a topic. You need to review your assigned org file present in the course-forum. You can report the errors mentioned above in the allotted corpus.
  2. You will review the files commit-wise and add the comment right where the error is. Error message should be descriptive enough. To view the file, go to the file, click on history tab, click on the commit you would like to review, look at the changes made in the file and comment inline.
  3. TAs will curate content based on your review, and mark those reviews as resolved.

5 Instructions

  1. This is a contributory activity and not a competitive one. Please coordinate with your fellow group members (of the same topic) and make sure there are no duplicates/redundancies
  2. This is the last bonus assignment, so make good use of it !!
  3. Only the teams who participated in the Content-Creation Assignment are allowed to participate in this.
